Heart disease is one of the leading causes of death across the world. It refers to a wide range of conditions that affect the heart and blood vessels. These conditions include coronary artery disease, heart attacks, heart failure, arrhythmia (irregular heartbeat), and valve diseases. The heart is the main organ that pumps blood throughout the body, supplying oxygen and nutrients to all parts. When the heart or its vessels do not work properly, it can lead to serious health problems and even death. According to the World Health Organization (WHO), about 17.9 million people die each year due to heart-related diseases.
